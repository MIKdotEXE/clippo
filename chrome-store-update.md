## Extension Description (max 132 chars for summary)

**Summary:**
Save your favorite YouTube moments. Clip timestamps, organize into collections, and sync across devices.

**Full Description:**

Clippo lets you save your favorite moments from any YouTube video with just a few clicks.

🎬 CLIP TIMESTAMPS
Set start and end points on any YouTube video. One click to mark the moment — your clip is ready.

📁 ORGANIZE COLLECTIONS
Group clips into categories and macro-categories. Find any saved moment in seconds with search and filters.

☁️ SYNC EVERYWHERE
Create an account and your clips follow you. Cloud backup keeps everything safe and accessible from any device.

🎨 MODERN DESIGN
Beautiful dark and light themes. Toggle between them with a single click. Clean, minimal interface that stays out of your way.

⌨️ KEYBOARD SHORTCUT
Press Ctrl+Shift+Y (or Cmd+Shift+Y on Mac) to instantly open Clippo on any YouTube video.

🔒 PRIVACY FIRST
We only store what you save. No tracking, no ads, no data selling. Your clips are yours.

HOW IT WORKS
1. Open any YouTube video
2. Click the Clippo icon on the video player (or press Ctrl+Shift+Y)
3. Set your start and end timestamps
4. Give your clip a title and category
5. Hit Save — done!

View and manage all your clips in the archive. Share clips with friends — they'll see the exact moment you saved, with a beautiful branded player.

Sign in with Google, GitHub, or Discord to sync your clips across devices.

Free forever. No account required to start saving clips.

Website: https://clippo.app
Privacy Policy: https://clippo.app/privacy/
